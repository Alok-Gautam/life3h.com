The Framework

Three Independent Core Drives

Just as an AGI system requires a Core Objective Function and Independent Core Drives to operate, so does the human brain — a Biological General Intelligence. Our Core Objective Function is to Survive. But survival alone doesn't explain what makes life feel good. That answer lies in three irreducible drives:

♡
1st H
Happiness

Fulfillment from the quantity, quality, and proximity of your social connections. Family, friends, community — the bonds that make life warm.

▲
2nd H
Highness

The drive for professional achievement, mastery, and growth. The exhilaration of building, creating, and being recognized for your craft.

✦
3rd H
Holiness

Inner peace through elevated consciousness. Meditation, stillness, presence — the quiet wholeness that no external success can replace.

The Equation

The Equation of Fulfillment

F = Ha + Hi + Ho

F is your Fulfillment Constant — unique to you. Ha, Hi, and Ho represent your individual capacity for Happiness, Highness, and Holiness. The ratio in which these drives exist within you is your F-Ratio.

Two people can have the same objective level of happiness, yet one feels deeply fulfilled while the other faces a massive deficit — because their F-Ratios are different. The framework doesn't judge which ratio is better. It illuminates your unique path to fulfillment by revealing where your deficits actually lie.

The Origin

A 20-Year Journey to One Equation

1999

Enrolled at IIT Kanpur. A 25-year alumni reunion revealed deep dissatisfaction among the accomplished — sparking the question: What truly makes a good life? Dropped out to find the answer.

1999 – 2003

Immersed in philosophy, quantum physics, mathematics, and spirituality — searching for the foundational principles of human fulfillment.

2003 – 2005

Master's in International Relations at JNU, broadening the lens from individual to collective well-being.

2005 – 2009

Four years in the Himalayas — leaving books behind, focusing on unlearning. Then IIM Ahmedabad for an MBA, bridging inner wisdom with worldly systems.

2019

While building Oltau.AI — an Artificial General Intelligence system — the Eureka moment arrived. The human brain is a Biological General Intelligence. The three independent core drives crystallized into the Life 3H Framework.

A journey that began in 1999 finally concluded in 2019. Philosophy, spirituality, and artificial intelligence converged into a comprehensive framework for a good life.
